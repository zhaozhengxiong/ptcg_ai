# II. A. D-00 9

*Part of: II. Card Text*

---

#### D-0 9
Attack cost
◆
This refers to Energy necessary for attacks. See the Energy marks on the left of the attack name on the
Pokémon card.

[Example 1] Attack, Unarmed (Slowking SM2+ 023/049)
If you have no cards in your hand, ignore all Energy in this attack’s cost.

[Example 2] Attack, Imittack (Kecleon BW8 046/051)
Choose 1 of your opponent’s Active Pokémon’s attacks. If this Pokémon has the necessary Energy
to use that attack, use it as this attack.
